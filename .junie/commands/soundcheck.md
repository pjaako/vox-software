Please perform a full soundcheck of the system. 
1. Verify that audio hardware is accessible and list available devices.
2. Check the status of the core services: `mpd`, `upmpdcli`, and `raspotify`.
3. Run `mpc status` to see what is currently playing.
4. Summarize the soundcheck results.
